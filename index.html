Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What are the core objectives of this dashboard initiative?

The system serves as a central operational point to coordinate peaceful advocacy demands. The primary targets include securing a total legislative and structural overhaul of national testing agencies, establishing stricter anti-leak infrastructure protection protocols, and demanding immediate senior administrative accountability for consecutive examination errors.

How can I safely support the peaceful march remotely?

You can use the automated Accountability Dispatch Center panel on the right side. It lets you send a formal constitutional complaint email directly to state representatives or copy the text framework to raise clean awareness on localized civic channels.

Is this student platform associated with any partisan political formation?

No. Academic infrastructure integrity and student safety are non-partisan constitutional duties. The platform is entirely run by students and citizen advocates, welcoming all cross-party democratic alliances to show solid tracking solidarity behind the upcoming generation.
